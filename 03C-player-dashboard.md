# 03C – Player Dashboard

Each player has a personal dashboard where they can manage their profile and view their football activity.

The dashboard is the main interface for players and guardians.

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Profile
Basic player information:

- First name
- Last name
- Birth date / birth year
- Country
- Player ID
- Profile photo (optional)

---

### 2. Teams

Shows the teams where the player currently belongs.

Information displayed:

- Club name
- Team name
- Role
- Active status

The player may belong to multiple teams depending on competition rules.

---

### 3. Competitions / Tournaments

Shows competitions where the player's teams participate.

Information displayed:

- Competition name
- Season
- Team participating

---

### 4. Matches

Shows:

- Upcoming matches
- Recent matches
- Match results

---

### 5. Player Statistics

Statistics collected from matches:

- Matches played
- Goals
- Assists
- Yellow cards
- Red cards
- Minutes played

Statistics are linked to the **Global Player ID**.

---

### 6. Invitations

Shows invitations from teams or competitions.

Player can:

- Accept invitation
- Reject invitation

---

### 7. Privacy Settings

Player or guardian can control visibility.

Options:

- Public
- Team only
- Private

---

### 8. Profile Corrections

Players or guardians can request corrections if:

- duplicate profile exists
- incorrect data
- club added wrong information

The system follows the **Fair Play principle**.
